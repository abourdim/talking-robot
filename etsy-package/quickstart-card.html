Talking Robot

Talk • Listen • Emote — a browser robot that talks back
No install • No account • 100% local • Chrome / Edge
1
🌐

Open the App

Double-click index.html in Chrome or Edge. The robot face appears instantly. No server, no install.

2
🎙️

Pick a Voice

Open the voice / language dropdown, choose one of your system voices (deep, bright, British, robotic…). This is how your robot will sound.

3
💬

Type & Speak

Type into the text box, click Speak (or press Ctrl/Cmd+Enter). The mouth animates in sync with the speech.

4
🎤

Listen & Transcribe

Click Listen, speak into your mic. Your words appear as a transcript. Enable auto-reply to make the robot respond out loud.

5
📡

Optional — Bridge to a micro:bit

Have a BBC micro:bit V2? Click Connect, choose the board in the browser prompt, and every word you type streams over Web Bluetooth. Pick any of the 10 emotion faces (😐 😄 🥺 😠 😂 😜 😱 🥳 🤖 😴) to change the mood instantly.
