Talking Robot • Classroom Guide

1
🌐

Open in Chrome or Edge

Double-click index.html. The robot face lights up. No internet, no install, no login.

2
🎙️

Pick a Voice & Language

Open the voice picker. Try a deep voice, a fast one, a British one. Your robot has a personality you choose.

3
💬

Type Something, Hit Speak

Type "Hello class!", click Speak (or press Ctrl/Cmd+Enter). The mouth animates, the robot talks out loud.

4
🎤

Click Listen & Speak Back

Press Listen, say something into your mic. Your words appear as a transcript. Flip on auto-reply and the robot answers you.

5
😄

Change the Emotion

Click any of the 10 emotion faces — 😐 😄 🥺 😠 😂 😜 😱 🥳 🤖 😴. The robot reacts instantly. Optional: connect a micro:bit and stream words to it.

Every great conversation starts with "hello".

Your robot is listening. Today, you teach it how to feel.
