Talking Robot

Browser BLE Control Panel for the BBC micro:bit V2 (optional)
v1.0.0 • Digital Download
Thank you for buying Talking Robot! You just grabbed a zero-install, zero-account, browser-local control panel for the BBC micro:bit V2 (optional). Everything runs on your machine — no cloud, no telemetry, no nonsense. This page walks you through the first three things to do.

First 3 Things To Do

1
Flash the firmware. Open makecode.microbit.org, switch to JavaScript, paste makecode.ts, click Download, and drag the .hex to your micro:bit V2. The board will show an X.
2
Open the app. Double-click index.html in Chrome or Edge. It loads locally. Click Connect, pick your board, watch the status dot turn green.
3
Play. Press 1…8 to hop between LEDs, Sensors, Emotion, Voice Input, Graph, 3D, Bench, About. Tap F for fullscreen graph. No board? Flip on Simulate Mode.
