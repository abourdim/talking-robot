🤖 Talking Robot — Talk • Listen • Emote. A browser robot for kids, classrooms, and curious makers.

Open one HTML file in Chrome or Edge and you have a friendly robot that speaks your words out loud, listens through your microphone, and reacts with 10 one-click emotion faces. Optional: bridge to a BBC micro:bit V2 over Web Bluetooth to stream your typed words to the board.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🗣️ TALK (text → voice)
  • Type any text, pick a voice and a language, click Speak
  • The robot says it out loud and its mouth animates in sync
  • Powered by the browser Speech Synthesis API (uses the voices installed on your OS)

🎤 LISTEN (voice → text)
  • Click Listen, speak into the mic, the transcript appears live
  • Optional auto-reply toggle — the robot speaks back automatically
  • Powered by the browser Speech Recognition API

😄 10 EMOTION FACES (one click each)
  😐 neutral · 😄 happy · 🥺 sad · 😠 angry · 😂 laughing
  😜 silly · 😱 shocked · 🥳 party · 🤖 robot · 😴 sleepy
  Clicking any emoji swaps the robot face instantly.

📡 OPTIONAL MICRO:BIT BRIDGE
  • Pair a BBC micro:bit V2 over Web Bluetooth
  • Typed words auto-stream to the board (via UART service)
  • Great for wearable badges, room displays, or STEM projects

⌨️ ONE KEYBOARD SHORTCUT
  • Ctrl/Cmd+Enter in the text field triggers Speak
  • Everything else is a button — no hidden combos

🌐 RUNS LOCALLY
  • No server, no install, no account — just open index.html in Chrome or Edge
  • Monolingual English app; voices can be in any language your OS has

## 10. FAQ snippets

**Q: Does this work on an iPhone or Safari?**
No. The Web Speech API and Web Bluetooth are only available on Chrome / Edge (desktop, or Chrome on Android). Please use a laptop or Chromebook.

**Q: Do I need a micro:bit?**
No. The TALK / LISTEN / EMOTE features work without any hardware. The micro:bit is only for the optional Bluetooth bridge.

**Q: Can my students use it offline?**
Yes. Once you have the ZIP, `index.html` runs entirely from your hard drive. No internet required.

**Q: Does the robot record my voice?**
No. The transcript is produced by your browser's Speech Recognition API and stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

**Q: Can I use it across my whole school?**
Yes — buy the Site License ($199) for up to 30 teachers at one campus, or the District License ($499) for multiple schools.
