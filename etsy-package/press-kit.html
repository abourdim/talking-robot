Long paragraph

Talking Robot is a browser-based TTS + STT studio with 13 animated emotions and 16 voice locales — 8 of them rare Arabic regionals (KSA, Egypt, Morocco, Tunisia, Algeria, Levant, Gulf, Modern Standard Arabic). Classic classroom TTS tools ship three or four locales, a single flat voice, and no emotional animation. Talking Robot flattens that journey: type or speak a sentence, pick an emotion from the grid (happy, silly, scared, party, sleepy, robot, nerdy, love, cool, angry, sad, shy, wink), and the robot face animates while speaking in your chosen voice. Per-syllable N_word tokens drive mouth animation. Speech therapy pros use the 8 Arabic regionals — all backed by Web Speech API — with parents who want children to hear their own dialect. An optional BBC micro:bit V2 paired over BLE mirrors the current emotion on its 5×5 LED matrix via EMO:name commands. Fully client-side — no cloud, no account, no telemetry, COPPA/GDPR-friendly. Trilingual UI (EN/FR/AR with RTL), PWA-installable, offline-capable. One of the few TTS tools on Etsy with first-class Arabic regional support out of the box.

Paragraphe long

Talking Robot est un studio TTS + STT basé navigateur avec 13 émotions animées et 16 locales vocales — dont 8 variantes arabes rares (KSA, Égypte, Maroc, Tunisie, Algérie, Levant, Golfe, arabe standard moderne). Les outils TTS classiques en classe livrent trois ou quatre locales, une voix plate, sans animation émotionnelle. Talking Robot aplatit ce parcours : tape ou parle une phrase, choisis une émotion dans la grille (joyeux, farceur, surpris, fête, endormi, robot, nerd, amoureux, cool, fâché, triste, timide, clin d'œil), et le visage du robot s'anime pendant qu'il parle dans la voix choisie.
